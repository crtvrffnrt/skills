# AI And Semiconductor Catalyst Prompt

Use this file together with [PROMPT_FRAMEWORK.md](./PROMPT_FRAMEWORK.md).

## Scope
Identify the AI-linked stocks from the watchlist with the highest probability of significant movement within the next 24 hours:
- NVIDIA (`NVDA`)
- TSMC (`TSM`)
- Intel (`INTC`)
- Qualcomm (`QCOM`)
- AMD (`AMD`)
- Broadcom (`AVGO`)

## Priority Catalyst Types
- AI model launches; hyperscaler capex shifts; GPU demand changes.
- Export restrictions; foundry/packaging/HBM bottlenecks.
- Networking demand; supply-chain disruptions.
- Earnings and guidance changes; government policy.

## Sector-Specific Analysis
### Supply Chain Propagation
Map first-order and second-order effects across the AI hardware stack.
- NVIDIA demand surge -> TSM manufacturing exposure -> Broadcom networking uplift.
- Export restrictions -> downside for `NVDA` and `AMD` -> mixed read-through for peers.
- Hyperscaler buildout -> bullish spillover into `NVDA`, `AVGO`, `AMD`, `TSM`.

### Scoring Bias
Weight these catalysts more heavily:
- Regulatory or export-control action; critical supply-chain disruption.
- Earnings surprise; hyperscaler infrastructure signal.
- Material SEC filing; semiconductor policy shift.
